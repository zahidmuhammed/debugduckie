Learn About Rubber Duck Debugging

How to use Debug Duck:

  • Interact with the duck: Click on the duck to get debugging suggestions.
  • Explain your problem: Talk to the duck about your code issues (even if just in your mind).
  • Rotate view: Click and drag to rotate the camera around the duck.
  • Zoom: Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out.

Sometimes explaining your problem out loud helps you find the solution!
